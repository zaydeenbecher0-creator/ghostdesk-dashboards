How this works
1. Add your details here once.
2. When a client says yes, go to Generate Package.
3. Fill in their brief — name, scope, retainer.
4. Click generate. Four documents appear: welcome email, intake form, contract/SOW, and a 6-step onboarding checklist.
5. Click "Save client & start tracker" to add them to your roster and begin tracking their onboarding progress.
6. Copy each document into your email client / e-signature tool as needed.
This tool generates documents only — nothing is sent automatically. You stay in control of every send.
